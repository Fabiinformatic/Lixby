Returns Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Right of withdrawal

In accordance with European e-commerce regulations, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase within 14 calendar days of receiving the product without any justification.

Lixby voluntarily extends this period to 30 calendar days from product delivery to facilitate returns.

Important note: This right does not apply to personalized products, consumables, or downloaded digital services.

2. Conditions for returns

Products eligible for return:

  • LixBuds One and accessories
  • Other products unused or with original packaging intact

Conditions the product must meet:

  • Be in perfect condition
  • Include all original accessories
  • Be accompanied by proof of purchase
  • Original packaging in good condition when possible

3. Return process

Step 1: Notification

Notify your intention to return the product within the established period by sending an email to returns@lixby.com with:

  • Order number
  • Reason for return
  • Contact details

Step 2: Authorization

You will receive a return authorization (RMA) along with shipping instructions.

Step 3: Shipping

Pack the product securely and send it to:

Lixby S.L. - Returns Department
Calle Principal 123
28001 Madrid, Spain

Step 4: Refund

Once the product is received and verified, we will process the refund within 14 business days.

4. Shipping costs

Free initial shipping: We offer free shipping on orders over €30.

  • Defective product → Lixby pays shipping
  • Change of mind → Customer pays shipping

5. Non-refundable products

  • Personalized products
  • Products with evident signs of use
  • Products damaged by customer negligence
  • Downloaded digital services
  • Activated subscriptions or memberships

6. Product exchanges

If you wish to exchange a product for a different model or color, you must first process the return and make a new purchase. You can also contact our support for assistance.

7. Partial refunds

Partial refunds may be applied if:

  • The product shows signs of misuse
  • There is damage caused by the customer
  • Restoration costs are required

8. Defective or damaged products

If you receive a defective product:

  1. Report the issue within 7 days of delivery
  2. Attach photographs of the problem
  3. Lixby will cover the shipping cost
  4. You can choose between replacement or refund

9. Warranty

Our products include the legal 2-year warranty for manufacturing defects.

For claims contact: support@lixby.com
