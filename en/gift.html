Frequently asked questions
When does the gift card arrive?
Within one hour after payment. If you enter someone else's email, it will be sent directly to that person.
How do I use the code?
In the Lixby store cart, you will see a field to enter the code. It is automatically applied as a discount.
Does the gift card expire?
Lixby gift cards have no expiration date. They can be used at any time.
Can it be used on more than one purchase?
Yes. If the card balance is greater than the order, the remaining balance will be available for future purchases.
What if I make a mistake with the email?
Write to lixbyinfo@gmail.com with your order number and we will fix it.
